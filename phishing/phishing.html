×

Report it as a phishing scam and delete it. In this scheme, scammers send out a bulk text message claiming that you have won a prize or your bank account has been compromised.

You receive a call from someone claiming to be from “Windows Technical Support” saying they found viruses on your computer and they need to access your computer to fix the problem. What do you do?

×

Ignore the request and drop the call. This technique is known as voice phishing, or vishing, in which cybercriminals make phone calls purporting to be from legitimate companies to steal personal and company information. If the user in the above example allows the hacker to connect to their PC, the hacker will be easily able to hack into the user’s system.
